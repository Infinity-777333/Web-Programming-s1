Onam: A Vibrant Celebration of Kerala's Harvest and Culture

Onam is the most important annual harvest festival celebrated by people of all communities in the Indian state of Kerala. This multi-day festival, occurring in August or September, commemorates the mythical homecoming of the benevolent King Mahabali, a legendary ruler of Kerala's golden age. More than just a harvest festival, Onam is a spectacle of Kerala's rich traditions, uniting people in a spirit of prosperity, cultural heritage, and joyous communal celebration.
