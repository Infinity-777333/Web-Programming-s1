College of Engineering Chengannur


VISION:To be a leading institution that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development, empowering students to become responsible global citizens and future leaders.

ABOUT US


The college of Engineering Chengannur(CEC),was established by the Government of kerala under the aegis of IHRD,in the year 1993.It has been approved by All India Council for Technical Education(AICTE) and affliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Techonological University (KTU).Ever since its inception,the college has maintained its eminence as a leading engineering college in Kerala.Being located in chengannur town,Alleppey district the college has got access to all means of transport,communication and lodging facilities.

ADMISSION PROCESS

Admission to the courses is on an annual basis on the All Kerala Common Entrance Examination conducted bt the controller of Entrance Examination,Government of kerala.The admission to the free/merit seats and management seats are the central allotment process conducted by controller of examination,Government of kerala.
